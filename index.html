About Me

Hello, I'm Shriyansh Sharma, currently a 1st-year student at Delhi Technological University (DTU). With a strong academic background, I achieved a remarkable 98.89 percentile in JEE Mains and secured an impressive 18,000 rank in JEE Advanced. Passionate about entrepreneurship, I'm on a journey of building a business venture while pursuing my studies. Beyond academics, I am deeply involved in education and mentorship. Alongside my studies, I run a YouTube channel where I share insights on various topics and engage with a growing audience. Recognizing the challenges students face, I offer mentorship programs for JEE aspirants, leveraging my own experiences to guide and support them in achieving their academic goals. Driven by a commitment to innovation and learning, I constantly seek new opportunities to grow both personally and professionally. I believe in the power of education to transform lives and am dedicated to making a positive impact in this world.

Mentorship Program

Welcome to our comprehensive mentorship program designed to support JEE aspirants at every step of their academic journey. I am Shriyansh Sharma, and alongside dedicated educators and professionals, we offer live Zoom sessions where students can delve into new concepts, seek academic guidance, and discuss any personal challenges they may face. Our sessions are interactive and tailored to address both academic doubts and personal development needs. In addition to live sessions, we provide a wealth of resources such as meticulously curated problem sheets focused on JEE Advanced preparation. These resources are designed to enhance problem-solving skills and ensure thorough preparation for the exams. Furthermore, our Telegram group serves as a hub where I personally engage with each student, providing personalized support and fostering a collaborative learning environment. At the heart of our mentorship philosophy is a commitment to empowering students with the tools they need to excel academically and grow personally. We believe in nurturing talent, building confidence, and preparing students not just for exams but for lifelong success in their chosen endeavors.
